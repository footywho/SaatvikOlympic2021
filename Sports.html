Sailing refers to the sport of using wind to power sails and propel the boat forward

Shooting, the sport of firing at targets of various kinds with rifles, handguns, ​pistols
      

Weightlifting

In volleyball, each team tries to score points by grounding a ball on the other team's court across a net

Football is an 11 player team game, each team attempts to get the ball in the other team's net to score

Rugby
